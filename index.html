Resume

Summary

I have worked in the Software Engineering industry for 20+ years in many kinds of roles and using many different technologies. I am extremely adaptable and have been relied upon to pick up new technologies/languages on an as-needed basis. I am used to working in a team as well as on solo projects and tasks.

Employment History

  • 2001-present: Senior Software Engineer at CommScope (formerly Pace MicroTechnology/ Latens Ltd / ARRIS).

I joined CommScope (then Pace Microtechnology) from university as a member of the hardware team working on broadband wireless solutions however that quickly developed into software engineering. My role has evolved over the past 20+ years, particularly in light of internal career moves to the security business (Latens) and the company's acquisition by ARRIS Inc and then CommScope.

I worked for a few years on embedded software (ASSEMBLY/C) in a development role, bringing new products to the market and supporting them in manufacturing and in the field during the immediate post launch period. Thereafter, I moved into a predominantly maintenance role supporting a wide range of hardware platforms and software architectures for many different markets worldwide. This included bug fixing but also involved developing new features to increase their lifespan.

In late 2012, I moved to the Latens division of the company to work on Linux based server applications (C/C++/Java/SQL/Redis) for Digital Rights Management (DRM).

In 2019, ARRIS was acquired by CommScope and I moved to working on web applications (Typescript/Angular/Docker) for managing Hybrid Fibre and Passive Optical Network (PON) devices. My day to day work covers software/component design, coding, testing and integration with back-end services/APIs. I work as part of a small team of engineers with similarly diverse skills where my work ranges from solo projects and features to intra and inter-team collaborations with a wider scope. I carry out peer code reviews and learning mentoring for others.
